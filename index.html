About Jennie

Jennie, born and raised between Iowa City and France has worked everywhere from Washington D.C., to the Virgin Islands. Finally making her way to The Bay Area, she accomplished a long time dream of living in S.F.. Her previous experience includes not only barber work but upscale woman's cuts, balayage, ombre, smudging techniques and hair sculpting. Jennie had been working in the financial district when she stumbled across the newly on the market for rent space. That day she went to the lawyer and five weeks later she opened Ladies and Gents!
